About Us

Where Movement Transforms Lives

Experience exceptional care at The Movement Clinic, Salem, where we are dedicated to helping you achieve lasting results. Located near Megawin Switch Gear Company and Game Changers Turf, our clinic serves the Salem area with a passion for empowering individuals through movement and personalised attention.

Our expert team of physiotherapists focuses on providing comprehensive support, guiding you on a journey to relief and full recovery. We believe in the power of movement to transform lives, offering a pathway to better health and well-being.

What We Offer

Our Services

Comprehensive physiotherapy treatments tailored to your individual needs and recovery goals.

🦴

Orthopaedic Physiotherapy

Treatment for joint pain, fractures, post-surgical recovery, arthritis, and musculoskeletal conditions.

🧠

Neurological Rehabilitation

Specialised therapy for stroke, Parkinson's disease, nerve injuries, and neurological disorders.

🏃

Sports Injury Rehabilitation

Get athletes and active individuals back to peak performance after sprains, tears, and sports injuries.

🧘

Posture & Chronic Pain

Assessment and correction of postural imbalances, back pain, neck pain, and chronic discomfort.

👴

Geriatric Physiotherapy

Gentle, effective care for elderly patients — improving mobility, balance, and quality of life.

🤰

Women's Health Physio

Pre- and post-natal care, pelvic floor therapy, and support through every stage of womanhood.
